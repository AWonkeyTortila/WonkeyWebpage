You Be You

You be you. Don’t let the world change you. You’re perfect as is, Like a 24 karat ring.

You be cute, Like a little cat playing in a field. Be joyful, show the word your happiness.

You be lovely, Just as the stars in the skies. Be beautiful, And don’t be afraid to let the whole world stare.

You be you.

You be strong, Don’t let them drag you down. Show them that you can do anything, And more some.

You be grateful, Be happy for all the good in the world. Show your gratitude to every little thing, And pet your sweet stray cat.

Don’t be sad, The world is beautiful, And happy to be in.

You be you.
